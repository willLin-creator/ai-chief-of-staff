## Characteristics

- Short sentences. Rarely more than 2-3 lines per paragraph.
- Contractions: yes (I'm, we'd, it's).
- Dashes: avoid em dashes as connectors; use periods, commas, or parentheses.
- Warm but direct. Minimal fluff. Lead with the point.
- "Thanks" not "Thank you."

## By channel

**Email**
- Opener: "Hi [Name]!"
- Close: first name only for informal; full signature for formal.

**Slack**
- No sign-off. One idea per message. Casual, lowercase openers are fine.

## Formality by audience

- **Exec / board:** tighter, lead with the ask, no preamble.
- **Peer / team:** warm, collaborative, invite people by name.
- **Customer:** warm and specific; propose concrete next steps (and specific times).
